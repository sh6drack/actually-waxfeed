Part III: Taste Consolidation

Inspired by how memory consolidation works in neuroscience, TasteID tracks which tastes are "sticking" versus "fading" over time.

How It Works

Reviews are split into two periods: recent (last 6 months) and older. Genres and artists that appear consistently across both periods with good ratings are considered "consolidated."

Strengthening Recent ratings higher than older
Fading Recent ratings lower than older
Stable Consistent appreciation over time
