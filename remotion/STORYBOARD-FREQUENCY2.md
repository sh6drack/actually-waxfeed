# FREQUENCY 2.0 - Storyboard

## Target: Gen Z (TikTok/Reels/Shorts)
## Duration: 30 seconds
## Vibe: Fast, punchy, relatable, slightly unhinged

---

## THE HOOK (0-3s) - 90 frames
**Visual:** Split screen of two people texting. One sends "what music are you into?"
**Text overlay:** "THE DREADED QUESTION"
**Energy:** Anxiety. Panic. We've all been there.
**Why it works:** Instantly relatable. Everyone has fumbled this.

---

## THE STRUGGLE (3-6s) - 90 frames
**Visual:** Rapid montage of:
- Spotify Wrapped screenshots (blurred/generic)
- "I listen to everything" text bubble
- Friend's disappointed face
- Awkward silence
**Text overlay:** "you can't explain your taste"
**Energy:** Cringe. Pain. Truth.

---

## THE RATE (6-10s) - 120 frames
**Visual:** Album art appears. Thumb swipes a rating (clean UI animation).
Rating: 8.5 ★
Another album. Swipe. 6.0.
Another. 9.5.
Fast montage of ratings stacking up.
**Text overlay:** "rate what you actually think"
**Energy:** Satisfying. Decisive. No algorithm BS.

---

## THE MAP (10-14s) - 120 frames
**Visual:** From the ratings, a taste fingerprint emerges.
Genre radar chart building in real-time.
Your archetype reveals: "THE CURATOR" or "THE EXPLORER"
**Text overlay:** "your taste. mapped."
**Energy:** Discovery. Self-knowledge. Cool data viz.

---

## THE MATCH (14-19s) - 150 frames
**Visual:** Your taste profile connects with others.
Lines drawing between you and compatible users.
Match percentage: 94%
Shared albums appearing between profiles.
**Text overlay:** "find your music people"
**Energy:** Connection. Community. Not alone anymore.

---

## THE PROOF (19-24s) - 150 frames
**Visual:** Quick cuts of:
- Review snippets from real users
- "finally someone gets it" comment
- Two users bonding over obscure album
- Notification: "3 new taste matches"
**Text overlay:** "12,000+ albums rated"
**Energy:** Social proof. FOMO. Activity.

---

## THE CTA (24-30s) - 180 frames
**Visual:** WaxFeed logo. Clean. Gold accent.
App preview or URL.
**Text overlay:** "rate albums. find your people."
**Final frame:** "waxfeed.com" + socials
**Energy:** Clear action. No confusion.

---

## AUDIO NOTES
- Trending sound or original beat
- Bass drop on THE RATE reveal
- Satisfying "ding" sounds on ratings
- Build to the match moment

---

## KEY DIFFERENCES FROM V1
1. **Opens with RELATABLE SCENARIO** not abstract concept
2. **Shows the PROBLEM first** (can't explain taste)
3. **Product is the SOLUTION** not just features
4. **Social proof** included
5. **Clear story arc**: Problem → Solution → Result → Action
